Eurovision Optical at 107 W 37th St will order ANY designer frame for you and bill VSP directly. Not a fancy office but this is the "girlmath free Bottega's" move. They'll order Gucci, BV, Gentle Monster, whatever — you just need your VSP info. Walk-ins welcome, Mon-Fri 9-5:30.

Blue Light Lenses

VSP covers standard single-vision or progressive lenses with $25 copay. Blue light coating (Crizal Prevencia, Eyezen, etc.) may be an extra $15-75 depending on your plan. Ask for it when ordering — most in-network providers offer it. The key lens brands for blue light: Crizal Prevencia, Zeiss DuraVision BlueProtect, Hoya Blue Control.
